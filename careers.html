NFT Digital Artist

Apply for this job

You will be working closely with the founders and Engineering to ensure that our product suite fulfills the company’s vision in a data-driven and agile way. You will help build and lead our team of Product Managers where competences, priorities and requirements vary between web2 and web3.

Your job will be to:

  • Manage and improve our Product Development process with a user centric focus
  • Work with the founders to define a product roadmap for the business
  • Be the voice of our users and service providers
  • Grow and manage a stellar product team
  • Manage stakeholder relationships
  • Own the roadmap of a product that becomes one of the TOP10 crypto projects in 3 years
  • Ensure high velocity of product development via dual track agile

Requirements:

  • 5+ years of working and leading product teams and functions
  • Experience (or working knowledge) in crypto, web3, blockchain, DeFi
  • Be comfortable with working in ambiguous environments where you need to find your own answers and solutions
  • Excellent stakeholder management skills
  • Excellent communication skills
  • Have the ability to express complex ideas simply and clearly
  • Proficiency in English both verbal and written
  • An interest in Metaverse is a plus


Meta x Land has built one of the largest metaverses in less than a year. You will be working with the best of the best and one of the keys for success is business focused creativity. If you are someone who fits the above criteria and thinking up new ideas to make a product better, more profitable and more addictive as a default, we’re looking for you.
